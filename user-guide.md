<h1>User Guide</h1>
<p><strong>GPS Camera App</strong></p>

<p>
Welcome to GPS Camera App.  
This guide will help you understand how to use the app to capture photos and videos with GPS location watermark.
</p>

<h2>1. Getting Started</h2>
<ol>
    <li>Install GPS Camera App from the Play Store.</li>
    <li>Open the app.</li>
    <li>Allow required permissions when prompted.</li>
</ol>

<div class="note">
<strong>Required Permissions:</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Camera – to capture photos and videos</li>
    <li>Location – to get GPS details for watermark</li>
    <li>Storage / Media – to save photos and videos</li>
</ul>
</div>

<h2>2. Capturing Photos</h2>
<ol>
    <li>Open the GPS Camera App.</li>
    <li>Wait for GPS to lock and location details to appear.</li>
    <li>Tap the <strong>Capture Photo</strong> button.</li>
    <li>The photo will be saved with GPS watermark.</li>
</ol>

<h2>3. Recording Videos</h2>
<ol>
    <li>Switch to <strong>Video Mode</strong>.</li>
    <li>Ensure location details are visible.</li>
    <li>Tap the <strong>Record</strong> button to start.</li>
    <li>Tap again to stop recording.</li>
</ol>

<h2>4. GPS Watermark Details</h2>
<p>
Each photo or video may include:
</p>
<ul>
    <li>Latitude and Longitude</li>
    <li>Address (based on GPS)</li>
    <li>Date and Time</li>
</ul>

<h2>5. Viewing Saved Photos & Videos</h2>
<ol>
    <li>Open your phone’s Gallery or File Manager.</li>
    <li>Navigate to the GPS Camera App folder.</li>
    <li>View, share, or delete files as needed.</li>
</ol>

<h2>6. Location Accuracy Tips</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Enable GPS / Location services on your device.</li>
    <li>Use the app outdoors for better accuracy.</li>
    <li>Wait a few seconds for GPS to stabilize before capturing.</li>
</ul>

<h2>7. Privacy & Data Usage</h2>
<p>
GPS Camera App does not upload or share your data.
All photos, videos, and GPS information remain stored locally on your device.
</p>

<h2>8. Troubleshooting</h2>

<h3>Location not showing?</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Check if Location permission is granted.</li>
    <li>Enable GPS in device settings.</li>
    <li>Wait for GPS signal.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Photos not saving?</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Check Storage / Media permission.</li>
    <li>Ensure sufficient storage space.</li>
</ul>

<h2>9. App Compatibility</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Android 12 and above</li>
    <li>Requires GPS-enabled device</li>
</ul>
