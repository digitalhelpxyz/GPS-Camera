<h1>About GPS Camera App</h1>

<p>
<strong>GPS Camera App</strong> is a smart camera application that allows you to capture photos and videos with
real-time GPS location details embedded directly into the media.
</p>

<p>
It automatically adds information such as <strong>latitude, longitude, address, date, and time</strong> as a watermark,
making your photos and videos more reliable, professional, and informative.
</p>

<h2>Key Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Capture photos and videos with GPS location watermark</li>
    <li>Automatic date and time stamp</li>
    <li>Accurate latitude and longitude details</li>
    <li>Address display based on GPS location</li>
    <li>Works offline after location is detected</li>
    <li>Simple, fast, and user-friendly interface</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why Use GPS Camera App?</h2>
<p>
GPS Camera App is ideal for site inspection, construction work, field surveys, travel documentation,
real estate, delivery proof, and personal memories where location accuracy matters.
</p>

<p>
All photos and videos are saved directly on your device.
The app does not upload or share your data with any server, ensuring full privacy and control.
</p>

<h2>Data & Privacy</h2>
<p>
GPS Camera App respects user privacy.
All processing is done locally on your device, and no personal data is collected or shared.
</p>

<h2>Compatibility</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Android 12 and above</li>
    <li>Supports modern camera and GPS hardware</li>
</ul>
