<div class="faq">
    <h3>1. What is GPS Camera App?</h3>
    <p>
    GPS Camera App allows you to capture photos and videos with GPS location, date, and time
    embedded directly as a watermark on your media.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>2. Does GPS Camera App work offline?</h3>
    <p>
    Yes. Once your GPS location is detected, the app can work offline.
    However, an internet connection may be required initially to fetch address details.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>3. Why does the app ask for location permission?</h3>
    <p>
    Location permission is required to add accurate GPS details such as latitude, longitude,
    and address to your photos and videos.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>4. Why does the app ask for camera permission?</h3>
    <p>
    Camera permission is required to capture photos and record videos using your device camera.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>5. Where are my photos and videos saved?</h3>
    <p>
    All captured photos and videos are saved locally on your device in the GPS Camera App folder
    and can be accessed via your Gallery or File Manager.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>6. Does the app upload my photos or videos?</h3>
    <p>
    No. GPS Camera App does not upload, store, or share your photos, videos, or location data
    with any server or third party.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">
    <h3>7. Can I remove GPS watermark from photos?</h3>

    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">

    </p>
</div>

<div class="faq">

<div class="faq">
