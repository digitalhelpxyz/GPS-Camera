Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

GPS Camera App respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store your personal files on our own servers.

  • Camera: Capture photos and videos.
  • Location (GPS): Add GPS coordinates, maps, weather and speed information.
  • Storage / Photos & Videos: Save photos, videos, PDFs and audio files.
  • Microphone: Record video audio and optional voice notes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Capture photos and videos.
  • Add GPS, date & time stamps.
  • Display maps, weather and speedometer.
  • Create PDF reports.
  • Record optional voice notes.
  • Save all generated content locally on your device.

Photos, videos, PDFs, GPS information and voice notes are not uploaded to our servers.
