Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GPS Camera App respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data on our own servers.

The app may access the following data on your device:

  • Camera: To capture photos and videos
  • Location (GPS): To add location watermark
  • Storage / Media: To save files
  • Microphone: For video recording

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Capture photos and videos
  • Add GPS location, date, and time watermark
  • Save files locally on your device

We do not upload your personal photos, videos, or GPS data to any server.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display ads.

These services may automatically collect certain information:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address

This data is used for:

  • Showing relevant ads
  • Improving ad performance
  • Preventing fraud

Learn more:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. User Consent (GDPR - EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we request your consent before showing personalized ads.

You may:

  • Accept or reject personalized ads
  • Change your consent anytime in app settings

We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage consent.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data sharing

We do not sell personal data. However, third-party services may process limited data for advertising.

To make a request, contact us via email.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data directly. Third-party services (like AdMob) may process limited data as described above.

7. Data Security

Your photos, videos, and GPS data remain stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal files.

8. Permissions Explanation

  • Camera: Capture photos and videos
  • Location: Add GPS watermark
  • Storage / Media: Save files
  • Microphone: Record audio in videos

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Your Choices
