Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is GPS Camera App?

GPS Camera App allows you to capture photos and videos with GPS location, date, and time embedded directly as a watermark on your media.

2. Does GPS Camera App work offline?

Yes. Once your GPS location is detected, the app can work offline. However, an internet connection may be required initially to fetch address details.

3. Why does the app ask for location permission?

Location permission is required to add accurate GPS details such as latitude, longitude, and address to your photos and videos.

4. Why does the app ask for camera permission?

Camera permission is required to capture photos and record videos using your device camera.

5. Where are my photos and videos saved?

All captured photos and videos are saved locally on your device in the GPS Camera App folder and can be accessed via your Gallery or File Manager.

6. Does the app upload my photos or videos?

No. GPS Camera App does not upload, store, or share your photos, videos, or location data with any server or third party.

7. Can I remove GPS watermark from photos?

The GPS watermark is added at the time of capture. Currently, it cannot be removed from already captured photos or videos.

8. Why is my GPS location not accurate?

GPS accuracy depends on device sensors and environment. For best results, use the app outdoors and wait for GPS to stabilize before capturing.

9. Is my personal data safe?

Yes. All processing is done locally on your device. GPS Camera App does not collect or share any personal information.

10. Does the app support video recording?

Yes. GPS Camera App supports recording videos with GPS location and time watermark.

11. Why is location not showing inside buildings?

GPS signals may be weak indoors. Try moving closer to windows or go outdoors for better GPS reception.

12. What Android versions are supported?

GPS Camera App supports Android 12 and above.

13. Does the app require internet?

Internet is not required for capturing photos or videos. It may be needed only for converting GPS coordinates into address text.

14. Can I share photos and videos?

Yes. You can share captured photos and videos directly from your Gallery or any sharing app.
